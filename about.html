About Us

Who We Are

Based in Pune, India. We are a team that works together to design, manufacture and supply a wide range of products. The products include Modular Kitchen, Kitchen Accessories, Home & Office Furniture.

What We Do

We accept orders from Dealers, Interior Designers, Architects, and personal projects. After designing the product we select appropriate high-quality material and manufacture it at the workshop. Our products are used in Homes, Offices, Restaurants and Hotels.

Why We Do It

We fully understand the need of our customers. In today's world, everyone has their own choice of designs according to their comfort, lifestyle and preferences and we truly appreciate that. Therefore at our company, we work on designs that are Modern, Durable and customizable.

How We make it possible

To start with the work we first visit the site and take the measurements. Then, with the use of modern software, we design the product that keeps your space, budget and storage needs in mind. Later we use our machinery to manufacture the product.
